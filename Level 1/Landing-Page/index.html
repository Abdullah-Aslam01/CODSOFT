Basics of Lovebird Genetics

Agapornis (Lovebirds) exhibit fascinating genetic traits. These small parrots have several color mutations, including blue, green, and yellow. Lovebird genetics follow a recessive pattern, meaning that two copies of a specific gene are needed to express a particular color.
